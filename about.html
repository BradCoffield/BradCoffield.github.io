Hi there.
I'm a librarian and web developer from Billings, MT.

I worked for Saint Francis University Library from 2006 to 2018 and now I work for Rocky Mountain College. My job's a lot of fun, and I do a variety of things: reference, instruction, collection development, and web development.

I love to make websites and work with code. Please take a moment to check out my work on library sites, personal webpages, and non-profits.

One of the reasons I love being a librarian is because of how much I enjoy helping people. I bring that same passion to my web work. If you're someone looking for a new website (or an update to an existing one) for yourself, your small-business, non-profit, or church, let me know. My freelance rates are reasonable and if you represent a small organization without many resources I'm also interested in donating my services. Get in touch!
